DAVAO ORIENTAL STATE UNIVERSITY

Davao Oriental State University (DOrSU) was established through Republic Act No. 11033, signed into law by President Rodrigo Roa Duterte at the MalacaÃ±ang Palace on May 28, 2018. Its conversion is an end-product of House Bill 7007 authored by Congress Representative Joel Mayo Z. Almario and Senate Bill 1617 filed by Senator Joel E. Villanueva. President Emeritus Edito B. Sumile successfully paved the groundwork for the University conversion and was sustained by his successor, Dr. Roy G. Ponce, later named the first University President.
